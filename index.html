SUGANDHA

Where aroma begins

Before food was tasted, it was smelled.
Before aroma filled kitchens, it crossed oceans.

Sugandha exists to preserve the original aroma of spices — not to sell, not to persuade, but to remember.

1. The Chilli Plant

Chilli is a fruit that grows on a plant. It begins green and turns red when mature. Everything that follows depends on this moment.

2. Chilli Varieties

Some chillies are grown for heat. Some for colour. No single chilli does everything honestly.

3. Whole Dried Chilli

This is the most transparent form of chilli. If truth is lost here, it cannot be recovered later.

4. Chilli Powder

Powder brings convenience, but hides identity. Trust becomes necessary once visibility is lost.

5. Chilli Flakes

Flakes preserve visibility. What you see is what you cook with.

6. Heat

Heat is sensation, not flavour. Controlled heat invites eating; aggressive heat ends it.

7. Colour

Colour creates expectation. Stability matters more than intensity.

8. Oleoresins

Liquid chilli exists to provide control in large-scale food, not to replace the plant.

9. Lab Truth

Tests exist to reduce arguments, not to replace judgement.

10. Export Reality

Export rewards clarity and punishes surprises.

11. Make Your Own Chilli Powder

The closest path to truth is grinding small quantities from whole chillies.
